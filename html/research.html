I've spent two summers doing both independent and team-based research projects at Bowdoin. Here's some of what I worked on:

During the summer 2011 research season, I worked with Prof. Daniela Oliveira to expand on research into a virtual-machine based protection system against TCP control-flow hijacking attacks. An abstract of this research is available here.

In the summer of 2012, I worked with Bowdoin's Northern Bites robotics soccer team to develop diagnosic and analyisis tools to improve robotic performance in overloaded wireless networks. This included on-site development at Robocup 2012 in Mexico City.
